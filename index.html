    About me

    Hao Chen , is a PhD candidate in the Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth (RADI), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). His research focuses on GIScience, remote sensing, and environmental modeling. More specifically, He is interested in applying various geospatial techniques to study Earth and Planetary Sciences, especially wetland dynamics and hydrology modeling.

    Education:

    2014 to present | Ph.D in Cartography and Geography Information System (RADI, CAS).

    2010 to 2014 | B.S in Geography Information System (Hefei University of Technology).
